About Us

The reality of maths not taught in the classroom.

Curiosity

Experimentation

Invention

Discovery

At Mathematika, we feel like the way maths is taught in the classroom is an injustice against the student, and fails to capture what maths is really about. We intend to bring you an environment where mathematics can not only be enlightening, but entertaining.



Unfortunately, this is what the education system does to students. By prioritising the answer, it ignores the core of mathematics. By prioritising the solution, it ignores the exploration, thought-process and invention behind its derivation. By never letting students experiment and play with maths, it turns the science into a chore.
