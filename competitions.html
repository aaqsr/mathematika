π-athalon

Can you gather your friends and prove that your problem-solving and logic skills are matchless, even with the threat of elimination lurking around every corner? It's 4 of you vs. the batch. (Teams can have 2-4 members)

Piathlon is one of the biggest events here at the Lyceum and it’s all about math and problem solving. Contestants compete in minor modules to acrew points, and top teams battle it out in the major so that we can crown our champion.

Minors

This year, to make Piathalon as accessible as possible, we’ve kept 4 minor modules spread all around the year, with points given to position holders. Your best 3 modules are counted so it’s okay if you mess up once. Each module is designed with the students in mind. And is less about brute force calculations and more about using your creativity and ingenuity to outwit the problems thrown at you. The Major

If your team is among the lucky few, who make it to the top by the end of the year, then pat yourselves on the back and get yourselves ready for the final showdown expected to be on campus.

This year the major is expected to be an intense buzzinga round, pitting the top teams against each other. But beware, not everything may be as it seems. Do you have the the lightning fast reflexes and the insane intellect to work with your team and beat your opponents to the bell, or will you be denied of the prize that you believe is so rightfully yours?
